Trump administration doesn't think about American farmers

Yong – 5 September, 2019

The Trump administration appears incapable of understanding the broader consequences of its policy decisions. The ongoing trade war with China harms American farmers and the government’s damage control measures reflect its ineptitude. The administration began a trade dispute with Beijing without a contingency plan for retaliation on the largest U.S. export commodity to China, soybeans. The administration sabotaged efforts by American farmers to find new markets for soybeans by engaging in trade disputes with prospective importers.
